Veev Panel Prototype

  • Prototype built with p5.js processing javascript library.
  • Reference for functionality only, not visual design.
  • Toggling developer mode hides overlaid annotations.

Functionality Notes

  • Touching and dragging anywhere in the devices column begins adjustment take over. Including the whole column as a potential touch area requires less precision and increases usability.
  • We need a good way to differentiate between this touch and drag gesture, on/off taps, and page swipes.
  • The first touch establishes the touch-origin and moving away from that point adjusts the setting from its current position.
  • Each Column has has a “Decrease Zone” (denoted in yellow in developer mode), which when a drag falls into this zone the adjustment behavior is reversed with the setting decreasing when moving away from the touch-origin and increasing as you move back towards it.
  • Also of note, the drag distance needed to reach 100% and fill the circle is smaller than the radius or the circle. The distance you drag and the percentage it increases should not be 1:1 and this ratio will probably be something we would like to tune in final testing.
